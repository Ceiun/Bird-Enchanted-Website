About

Birds Enchanted is a public sharing website where you can share
your love for birds to your hearts content. You do not have to
know everything about birds, simply open minded, as your time
here may even educate you.


Share your creativity, your story and get inspired!



















Our Story

Found in September of 2019, making a website was once only
a happy thought for Lilia Lim, the founder of Birds Enchanted.
Having always being interested in learning coding and being
surrounded by few peers with similar interest towards birds as
her, Lilia opened Birds Enchanted to see just how many more
people shared the same interests as she did for the little creatures.
